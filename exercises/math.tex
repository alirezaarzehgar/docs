Simple derivative formula is: $(aU^n)' = na U^{n-1} U'$

\begin{align*}
	Q_{n,0} &= 1   \quad Q_{0,k} = [k=0];  \\
	Q_{n,k} &= Q_{n-1,k}+Q_{n-1,k-1}+\binom{n}{k}, \quad\text{for $n$, $k>0$.}
\end{align*}

(\tan U)' &= U'(1+\tan^2 U) \\
f(x) &= \lim_{x\to\inf} \frac{x^2 + \sqrt[3]{3x^2}}{x-1} \\
\end{align*}
